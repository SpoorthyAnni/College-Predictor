ABOUT US

College Admission Predictor System is web based application which predicts the admission in colleges based on the ranks entered by the students. The students are required to choose the city where they want to apply and enter their examination ranks. Based on the information entered by the students, the colleges along with their branches are displayed. The student can view the connectivity, facilities, ranking, fees, placement details and past recruiters of each college. There is a need to forecast the current trend in the admissions and job sectors so as to blend the courses and syllabus accordingly to keep the youth employed and skilled with rapidly changing world. Hence, our goal is to create an website which helps the student to choose the right college during his/her seat allotment process.
